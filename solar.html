Battery Information

This panel displays real-time information about your battery status:

  • Percentage: Current charge level (0-100%)
  • Voltage: Battery voltage in volts (V)
  • Current: Charging/discharging current in amps (A)
  • Power: Instantaneous power in watts (W)
  • Temperature: Battery temperature in Celsius

When charging, the percentage will increase and the current will show a positive value. When discharging, the current will be negative.

Load Management

This section would display information about connected loads and power consumption:

  • Connected Devices: List of devices drawing power
  • Power Consumption: Total load in watts
  • Priority Settings: Configure which devices get power first
  • Load Shedding: Automatic disconnection when battery is low

In a real system, you could turn devices on/off from this panel to manage your power usage.

System Statistics

Historical data and performance metrics:

  • Daily Generation: Solar power generated today
  • Monthly Trends: Performance over time
  • Efficiency: System conversion efficiency
  • Battery Health: Capacity degradation over time

This data helps you understand your system's performance and identify any issues.
